Odd

ODD is a fashion practice, which has no shape yet. It takes different shapes with, me Ishara Jayathillake. But whatever I do, I always feel ODD.

Ishara Jayathilake

I am a fashion designer from Sri Lanka, graduated in 2014 from Academy of Design, Colombo with a BA in Fashion Design from Northumbria University, UK. ODD is my personal fashion practice. It has no clear shape yet. But I am exploring different avenues. I have a strong understanding of Sri Lankan traditional craft and love for sophisticated construction of garments. I work on custom design and manufacturing for people who share my odd flavour.
